Transparency

Listing policy draft

VisitGensan.com is an independent visitor guide. Listings are created from public information, local research, submitted corrections, and verified business details when available.

Paid placements should always be marked as Sponsored, Featured Partner, or Paid Placement. Editorial listings are not official rankings and should not be presented as government-approved or business-endorsed unless clearly confirmed.

Business details may change. Visitors should confirm prices, hours, availability, and booking terms directly with the business before visiting or reserving.

Business owners

Claim a listing

If you own or manage a listed business, contact VisitGensan.com to request corrections or claim the listing. Verification may require a business email, official page proof, phone confirmation, or manual review.

Claimed listings may later allow owners to submit updated photos, contact details, amenities, promos, and corrections.
