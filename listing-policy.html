About the Creator

Mark Ryan Wamilda

Hi, I’m Mark Ryan Wamilda, the creator of VisitGensan.com. I built this local guide to help visitors and residents discover useful places around General Santos City, from hotels, restaurants, parks, and attractions to practical travel tips.

My goal is to make GenSan easier to explore by sharing helpful information, local notes, photos, and links to official pages whenever available. VisitGensan.com is also my personal tribute to the city I proudly call home.

As an independent local guide, some details may change over time. Readers are encouraged to contact each business or destination directly for the latest schedules, rates, and availability.

Listing Policy

VisitGenSan.com is an independent local travel guide featuring places, businesses, experiences, and stories connected to General Santos City and nearby destinations.

Listings may be created from public information, local research, community recommendations, or details submitted by business owners and contributors. All submissions are reviewed before publication, and submitting information does not guarantee inclusion.

We aim to provide helpful and accurate information, but business details such as operating hours, prices, contact information, availability, and services may change. Visitors should confirm important details directly with the business before visiting or making a reservation.

Business owners may contact VisitGenSan.com to request corrections or provide updated information. VisitGenSan.com reserves the right to edit, update, decline, or remove content when necessary.
